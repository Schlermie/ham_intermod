Known Problems & Solutions:

  1. If every channel is identified as a backup channel, then execution will fail with Server Error 500.
    Workaround: Add a dummy simplex channel with a frequency that won't cause IMD. Leave the Backups column blank for the dummy channel.
    For example: RxFreq=999, TxFreq= , Backups= , Label=Dummy
